About Me

A little about who I am and what I do

Who I Am

I'm a frontend developer with 4 years of experience crafting clean, user-friendly web interfaces. I care deeply about accessibility, performance, and writing code that other developers actually enjoy reading.

Before coding, I studied graphic design — which is why I pay close attention to typography, white space, and visual hierarchy. I believe great design and great code go hand in hand.

When I'm not at my desk, you'll find me hiking in Marin County, experimenting with sourdough recipes, or reading dense sci-fi novels.

My Approach

I start with the user. Every decision — from button placement to color choice — should serve the person on the other side of the screen. I then write semantic HTML, layer in CSS that works without JavaScript, and add JS only where it genuinely improves the experience.
